American Cancer Society

Tuesday Brunch | Rm 109

Hi we are Lynbrooks' American Cancer Society. Our mission is to raise awareness in the community and fundraise for cancer research and treatment. As a club, we participate in several walks a semester. Our biggest event is Relay for Life, which is a 24 hour event where we sell baked goods, eat a lot of good food, and participate in many fun activities to pass the time. Hope to see you all in room 109 on Tuesday brunches!
