Agape Christian Club

Wednesday lunches | Rm 72

Agápē (a·ga·pe) means "unconditional love". It is a love that is pure and true, free of any ulterior motives. This is the love that our God has for us--each and every single one of us.
Agape Christian Club is a loving community of Christians, spreading God's love on campus. Weekly meetings are held in the choir room (72) Tuesdays at lunch! Everyone is welcome. We love you!
