Aikido Martial Arts

Friday Lunch and Friday afterschool | Rm 202

Interested in Japanese culture? Curious about martial arts? Well look no further, Aikido club is the club for you! Explore the way of the warrior and Japanese culture in general during the lunch meetings, and learn first-hand the art of Aikido from Mr. Seike. Practices are Friday after school, from about 3:15 to 5:15. No prior martial arts experience necessary. Only requirement is an open mind and a sense of humor.

Officer Team: President: Daniel Li, VP: Leslie Sern, Treasurer: Judy Schel, Secretary: Alie Wu
