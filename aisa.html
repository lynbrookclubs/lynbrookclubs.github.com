AISA

Wednesday Brunch | Rm 410

Ethnic club for activities involving Indian Culture. Every year we host a Indian culture showcase called Silsiliay, inviting dance teams from all over the area to come and perform. All proceeds go to a charity called Project Rishi, benefiting a shelter called Anandwam for people diagnosed with leprosy.
