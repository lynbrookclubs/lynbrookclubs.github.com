Amnesty

Wednesday lunches | Room 204

This year, we plan on educating people more about (lack of) human rights and other difficulties that people from other parts of the world face. Members have opportunities to gain service hours through local and international activities such as helping out at homeless shelters, delivering care packages, working at soup kitchens, and sending letters to people in need such as refugees.
