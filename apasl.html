Asian Pacific Association of Student Leaders (APASL)

Monday brunch | Room 408

The goal of APASL is to expose high schoolers to leadership opportunities within their communities and build soft skills through youth conferences, workshops, and volunteer opportunities. In order to fulfill these goals, APASL is also a Team HBV branch of the Asian Liver Center at Stanford University.
