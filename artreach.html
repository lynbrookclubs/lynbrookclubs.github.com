ArtReach

Tuesday Brunch | Rm 408

Lynbrook ArtReach is a non-profit organization that contributes to the community through creating art. Our club stands out amongst other organizations on campus because you can explore an interest while receiving community service hours. Through ArtReach, we want to impact and create change in our community with our art by donating our crafts to local organizations and charities. No artistic experience is needed! We will provide instructions and all materials at weekly club meetings. The projects are then made either at after school socials or at home.
