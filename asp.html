Animal Services and Protection

Monday Brunch | Rm 212

The club goal is to advocate for animal welfare. We are associated with many organizations that hold adoption events for certain animals (dogs, cats, rabbits, etc.). We also encourage members to present on topics involving endangered species, environmental studies, and animal abandonment/abuse.
