Astrophysics Club

Wednesday lunches | Room 603

Astrophysics Club is a society in which we explore and indulge in the principles and fundamentals that rule our cosmos. We prepare for and participate in the USAAAO, one of the national science olympiads (similar to competitions such as USAPhO and USABO). We host a variety of interesting and engaging events including presentations by NASA officials, star parties (with free food!), internship information/postings, field trips, and hands-on activities.
