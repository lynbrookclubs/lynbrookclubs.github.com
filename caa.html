CAA (Conservation Action Association)

Tuesday brunch | Rm 77 (Band Room)

"We need YOUR help to save the planet!
CAA is dedicated to preserving our environment one footprint at a time. We are looking for members who are passionate about environmental issues and are willing to take actions to fight climate change. Our club is involved in a variety of activities, from beautifying parks to protecting the well being of animals. Not only do we provide service to our community, but we also have fun at many different socials throughout the school year.
Join CAA for an experience you'll never forget!"
