Creative Game Design

Tuesday Lunch | Rm 213

Creative Game Design (CGD) is a club focused on teaching students to understand, plan, and develop games. We use the Unity Game Engine as a platform to demonstrate each step of game design. There's a lot that goes behind the scenes of the games we play everyday, from Call of Duty to League of Legends, and in this club we'll explore these aspects in a greater depth. Our goal is to give members the knowledge and confidence to develop their own games!
