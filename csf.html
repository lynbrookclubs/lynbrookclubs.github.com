CSF (California Scholarship Federation)

Every other Friday Brunch | Room 301

CSF (California Scholarship Federation) is the oldest honor society in California with over 1,400 chapters statewide. CSF upholds high standards of volunteerism and academic achievement for all its members. It is the only honor club for which any Lynbrook student, even Freshmen and Sophomores, can apply. If the point requirements are met by the end of their Senior year, the members receive stoles during graduation.
