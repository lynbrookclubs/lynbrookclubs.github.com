Future Business Leaders of America (FBLA)

Thursday lunches | Room 506

FBLA gives students the opportunity to go beyond the classroom & acquire skills that will prove invaluable in the future. With career training, leadership development, & exciting competitions, FBLA offers real-world experience to the leaders of tomorrow. The official mission of FBLA-PBL is to bring business and education together in a positive working relationship through innovative leadership and career development programs. Regardless of whether or not you are interested in business, we highly encourage you to give it a try and become a part of our family.
