Free the Children

Friday lunches | Room 106

We encourage leadership and service by providing all club members mentorship and guidance in starting their own fundraising or awareness projects about causes they are passionate about, including but not limited to helping underprivileged kids and the homeless. We are also working towards fundraising for a school in Ecuador, for which we have raised over $5000 so far.
