Global Hunger Initiative

Thursday brunch | Room 212

Hunger is a constant issue in this world yet it is challenging to completely get rid of. Our goal is to help out others who are hungry by making meals and donating them to those in need. We also volunteer at local non-profit organizations to connect specifically with those in need.
