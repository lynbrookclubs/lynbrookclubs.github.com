GSA (Gender and Sexuality Alliance)

Tuesday lunch | Rm 506

GSA (Gender and Sexuality Alliance, formerly known as Gay-Straight Alliance) is a student-run club dedicated to discussing LGBT+ related topics and providing a safe space for all Lynbrook students. Typical meetings include presentations, discussions, debates, and other activities focusing on LGBT related issues. Many members just drop by for the weekly meetings, but we also offer a range of opportunities to get involved with the outside LGBT community beyond Lynbrook. Everyone is welcome to join GSA regardless of sexual orientation or gender, and there is free food at every meeting!
