HEAL

Thursday Brunch | Rm 210

Health Educational Association of Lynbrook is dedicated to encouraging students to develop leadership and technical skill competencies by providing community service opportunities, guest speakers, hospital tours, and other health-field related events. Our mission is to educate members about the different health professions available and have members gain hands-on experience with the health-professional world.
