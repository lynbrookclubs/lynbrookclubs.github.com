Hiking and Environmental Impact Club

Wednesday Brunch | Rm 409

The main mission of HEI is to nurture awareness and love for the environment in LHS and the community, while letting students participate in activities such as hiking, our annual Green Conference, Earth Week, the Electric Vehicle Show, environmental craft activities, the "Youth for a Cool Earth" program, fruit-picking community service opportunities for people in need, Full Circle Farm volunteer events and more!
