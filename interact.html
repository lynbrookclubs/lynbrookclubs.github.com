Interact

Friday Lunch | Room 509

Though Interact is a service club, you'll find more than just weekly volunteering opportunities by joining. Every year, we work with schools throughout the Bay Area to combat two problems we find in our nation and one on a global scale. This give you countless opportunities to get involved as either a committee member, officer, or even as a chair on the District Council. Interact is a club for those dedicated to making a difference in their community while meeting new people along the way to help you achieve your goals.
