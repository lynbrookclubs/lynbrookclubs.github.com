JETS (Junior Engineering Technical Society)

Friday Brunch | Rm 410

JETS, Junior Engineering Technical Society, is an on campus club that strives to encourage the application of STEM topics into real world situations. Our goal is to immerse students into an environment of deeper learning and understanding of current-day topics as well as their personal favorites. Through the use of club presentations, groups projects, hands-on activities, competitive events as well as member discussions, we hope to provide a memorable and informative engineering experience.
