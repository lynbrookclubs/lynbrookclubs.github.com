Junior Taiwanese American Student Association

Every other Friday brunch | Rm 104

The mission of JTASA is to share Taiwanese culture, history, and current events and to help empower young Taiwanese American citizens. We are connected to other JTASA chapters at schools like Cupertino, Irvington, and Mission San Jose, who we work with on community service projects. Additionally, JTASA hosts fun, social events like pearl milk tea making, which allows members to interact and network with people outside of the Lynbrook community.
