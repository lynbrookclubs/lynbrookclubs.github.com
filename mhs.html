Math Honor Society

Tuesday lunch | Rm 403

The purpose of this organization is to promote interest in mathematics, explore facets of mathematics not covered in school, from modular arithmetic to graph theory to combinatorial geometry, investigate fun aspects of mathematics including puzzles and riddles, and foster enthusiasm for math by creating friendly in-club competition as well as participating in various competitions outside of school.
