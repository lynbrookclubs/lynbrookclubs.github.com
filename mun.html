Model United Nations

Wednesday lunches | Room 211

A simulation of the United Nations, Model UN aims to educate members about international issues, public speaking, and current events to crafting globally conscious leaders. We attend conferences every year, on both local and national levels, where participants represent countries and work together in committee to discuss global affairs and solve relevant international crises. Topics range broadly, stretching from medicine and the ethics of science to human rights and business.
