National Honor Society

Every other Thursday Lunch | TBD

Must apply and be accepted to be part of the club. Applications are only available to Juniors and Seniors.
The National Honor Society (NHS) is the nation's premier organization established to recognize outstanding high school students. More than just an honor roll, NHS serves to honor those students who have demonstrated excellence in the areas of scholarship, leadership, service, and character. Lynbrook NHS is a branch of the national organization at Lynbrook High School based in San Jose, California.
