Philosophy Club

Monday Lunch | Room 511

We strive to explain how the world works and how different ways of thinking interact in our complex world. From discussing philosophers like Nietzsche to Kant to analyzing modern day policies like gun control and organ procurement, we host fun events and educational meetings to discuss philosophical paradoxes.
