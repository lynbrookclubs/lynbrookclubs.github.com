Pre-Medical Club

Wednesday lunches | Room 407

Our purpose is to educate our members about the fields of biology and medicine through informative presentations, guest speaker events, and practical applications of medical knowledge, in order to influence them to pursue a career in medicine. We hope that we can help all of our members to not only become more knowledgeable about medicine, but also develop a passion for it.
