Robotics

Wednesday Lunch | Rm 608

Lynbrook Robotics, FIRST Team 846, provides students with opportunities to excel in science, technology, and business. Each year, we design, fabricate, and code a 120 lb. robot, and compete at regionals across the nation through FIRST Robotics Competition. There are also many other aspects of our club that members can get involved in, ranging from graphic design and writing to public relations and presenting. Any member can find a part of robotics to enjoy.
