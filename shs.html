Spanish Honor Society

Friday brunches | Room 104

"SHS is an honor society for high school students taking Spanish who have shown an interest and passion in the language. During our meetings, we learn more in-depth about the Spanish language and different things about the culture. We also have cultural events and meetings with other schools' SHS .
*NOTE* Spanish Honor Society requires a teacher nomination to join and is only open to Sophomores and above"
