Web Dev Club

Tuesday Lunches | Rooms 605 & 606

We're a community for members to build their passions using web technologies. We will teach you how to build your very own websites and web apps, whether you're just starting out or already a code ninja. Our weekly lessons cover HTML, CSS, and Javascript, and we have two paths - one for backend and one for frontend. Our lessons are project-oriented, and by the end of the first semester you'll be able to build a nice-looking personal website (frontend) or even an interactive blog (backend). Come check us out!
