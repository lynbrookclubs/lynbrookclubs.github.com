Youth Activities for Kids

Thursday brunch | Rm 007

YAK is a community service club that focuses on interacting and socializing with children. What makes YAK unique is the level of engagement with kids at our events. We offer a variety of events, such as exchanging pen pal letters with the first graders at Dilworth Elementary, babysitting children at John Muir elementary, and reading books to kids at various libraries. Come out and experience for yourself!
